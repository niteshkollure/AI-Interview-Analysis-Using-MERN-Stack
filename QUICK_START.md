**Q: Can I take the same interview twice?**
A: Yes! Each time you get different random questions.

**Q: Can I skip questions?**
A: Yes, but skipped questions score 0. Better to try!

**Q: Do I need to complete all 4 modules in sequential mode?**
A: Yes, sequential mode goes through all 4 automatically.

**Q: Can I use voice recording?**
A: Yes, if your browser supports Web Speech API.

**Q: How long does one interview take?**
A: Sequential mode: 30-45 minutes (20 questions)
